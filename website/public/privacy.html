Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version urlStash collects no personal data whatsoever. Everything stays on your device. We have no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no accounts.

Overview

urlStash is a Chrome browser extension that helps you organize, stash, and restore browser tabs using nested folders. It operates entirely offline and locally within your browser.

This privacy policy applies to the urlStash Chrome Extension and its companion website at urlstash.onrender.com.

Data we collect

We collect nothing. Specifically, urlStash does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share:

  • Your browsing history or tab URLs
  • Personal information of any kind
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Crash reports or telemetry
  • Any data that leaves your device

Local storage

urlStash uses Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API solely to save your stashed tabs, folder names, and theme preference. This data:

  • Exists only on your local device
  • Is never synced to any external server
  • Is never read by us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the extension

Permissions used

urlStash requests the following Chrome permissions, each used strictly for local functionality:

  • tabs — to read the URLs and titles of your open tabs when you stash them or save a session state
  • activeTab — to read the currently active tab when you click "Stash Page"
  • storage — to save your stashed folders and settings locally on your device

None of this information is transmitted anywhere. It stays entirely within your browser.

Third-party services

urlStash does not integrate with, load from, or communicate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

Children's privacy

urlStash does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Since we collect no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
